* Data Jobs Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard analyzing 478K+ job postings from 2024 to explore trends, salaries, and job market insights across data roles.

* Overview

Built to answer practical questions for anyone exploring a career in data:

- What salary can I expect as a Data Analyst, Engineer, or Scientist?

- Which platforms post the most data jobs?

- How many roles require a degree or offer remote work?

* Features

- KPI cards — total job count, median yearly & hourly salary, salary rating

- Trend line — job posting volume across 2024 by month

- Scatter plot — hourly vs yearly salary comparison by role
  
- Bar chart — highest paying jobs in data
- 
- Drill-through page — click any job title to see its full breakdown (salary range, WFH %, health insurance %, hiring platforms, country map)

* Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- Data Modeling
